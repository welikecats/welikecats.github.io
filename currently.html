Current Events

Rio 2016

  • Great Britain's trampoline team made history at Rio when as they had never before qualified trampolinists to an Olympic Final.
    Both Kat Driscoll and Bryony Page qualified for the Olympic Final with Bryony Page taking home a surprise Olympic Silver Medal!

  • Canadian Gymnast Rosie MacLennan also made history at Rio by retaining her Olympic title from London.
